Acupressure Massage

For Beginners and Graduated Therapists

This beginner’s course in Acupressure is ideal if you are already practicing body massage or other bodywork therapies. The course will extend your skills to encompass a better understanding of trigger points throughout the body.

Acupressure Massage is an ancient healing art that uses the fingers to press key points on the surface of the skin to stimulate the body’s natural self-healing. When these points are pressed using the proper technique, they release muscular tension and promote the circulation of blood and the body’s life force to aid healing.

Learn how to give simple but highly effective Acupressure Massage to friends and family while locating and utilising Acupressure points. You can also use the information from this course for self-treatment, reducing a variety of negative body symptoms or pain.

Additionally, you’ll learn how Acupressure treatment can help to alleviate common ailments or pain from injuries. You will learn all of the Meridians in the body and how to locate the trigger points that rest along them. You will also get hands-on training for Acupressure point techniques required to deliver an effective treatment for your client.

These techniques improve your potential to expand your client base, giving you a large range of service beyond a simple relaxing massage. You will be able to confidently treat your clients with muscular pain and tenderness, lack of flexibility and other ongoing issues.
