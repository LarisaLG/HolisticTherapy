WHO IS THIS FOR

ALL LEARNERS WELCOME

For students new to massage therapy and holistic wellness, this course gives all the important details students need to have when they first start out. The course introduces all concepts related to massage therapy included the holistic benefits it offers clients. This introductory course provides a solid opportunity for curious parties who want to learn the basics of massage. For more serious students, it offers the core learning objectives prospective therapists need for a bright future in massage therapy.

Beyond the introductory courses, participants can also take advantage of advanced instruction for beauty and complementary therapy. These workshops address massage therapy and multiple related areas including beauty, physical therapy, acupuncture, and other health professionals. These complementary therapy processes will add even more of an impact for participants.
