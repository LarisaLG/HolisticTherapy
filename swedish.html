Swedish Massage

Postgraduate Training for Complementary Therapists

The Swedish Massage workshop is a beginner’s course which means you don’t need to have any previous qualifications to get started. It is open to everyone with an interest in learning how to give a full body massage.

Effective Swedish Massage Therapy offers clients a wealth of physiological and psychological benefits and can affect all the body systems in a positive way. Massage has an immediate physiological effect on the local area of the body where it is applied. It also affects the whole body through stimulation and relaxation of the muscles and the nerves.

Swedish Massage is one of the most popular techniques practiced throughout the world. Your training will teach you how to apply it for all client needs – from gentle and relaxing to vigorous deep tissue therapy.

From localised pain relief to relaxation and improved holistic wellness, there are enormous client benefits to regular Swedish Massage. You’ll learn how these benefits can improve your own life (or those of your clients if you’re a practitioner). You will also be introduced to the concept of a Holistic Lifestyle with tools on how to make positive changes that will benefit your health.
